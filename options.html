Our Features & Functions

Autovote

Automatically upvotes (updubs) each song at the beginning of it's play.

AFK Autorespond

Automatically responds in chat when mentioned with "I am AFK at the moment.", this function has a cooldown to prevent spam. Also customizeable.

Emotes

Allows the use of Twitch.tv, BTTV, and Tasty emotes in the chat. We recommend also having Autocomplete Emoji enabled in order to see the list of emotes when you begin typing the name. Example - :emote:

Autocomplete Emoji

Brings up a list above the send-chat box with all of the emoji's (and Twitch.tv emotes if you have them enabled). Start typing at least 3 letters to have the list come up.

Autocomplete Mentions

Brings up a list above the send-chat box with all of the usernames in the community. Start typing at least 1 letter to have the list come up. You can navigate through the list with the up & down arrow keys.

Notifications on Mentions

Notifies you in a pop-up when you are mentioned on Dubtrack. The pop-up will disappear after 5 seconds..

Fullscreen Video

The video takes up the entire screen. Press 'esc' or 'F11' on your keyboard to x-out of the fullscreen.

Split Chat

Separates each message box sent by a user with a lighter color. This allows messages to be easily identified and not seen as a huge clump of text.

Hide Chat

Removes the chat and leaves only the video section on the screen. The video is centered and expanded.

Hide Video

Removes the video and leaves only the chat section on the screen. The chat is centered and it's width is extended.

Hide Avatars

Removes the profile pictures/images in the chat, top header, and bottom header.

Hide Background

Completely hides the community's background leaving only the grey landscape. If you would rather have your own background, use our Custom Background feature.

Spacebar Mute

Allows the option to mute the sound on your page by pressing the spacebar on your keyboard. To unmute just press the spacebar again.

Show Timestamps

Permanently shows the timestamps for the messages in the chat.

Warn on Navigation

Warns your page when refreshing/leaving to make sure you want the page to be updated/changed.

plug.dj Theme

This is a custom theme created by Kris and redone by sinful. It resembles plug.dj, using it's colors and perhaps in the future, some of it's chat icons.

Community Theme

Enables the custom theme that the community you're in hosts in their room info. It is advised that you do not have this feature and the plug.dj Theme enabled at the same time, due to conflicting CSS.

Custom CSS

Insert your own (or someone else's) custom .css file into the popup-box when you click on the function, then press enter. This will change how you view Dubtrack.fm, whether colors, sizes, or positioning were edited/changed.

Custom Background

Insert your own (or someone else's) image file, preferably .jpg, into the popup-box when you click on the function, then press enter. This will overlay the community's background with one that you have chosen.

Bug Report

If you have any bugs to report to us for our script, use this feature to send them in. Thank you!

Install

Installing our extensions is easier than slapping a stranger.

  • Grab our old friend DubX bookmarlet here and drag it to the bookmarks bar.
  • Go to Dubtrack.fm.
  • Click on the DubX bookmark and you're done! In record time too.

Support

You will be able to rock with our extension running.

Once you install our extension you will get access to:

  • Autovote:

    Will click the UPDUB button automatically whenever the song changes.

  • Fullscreen:

    We don't all fancy backgrounds. Fullscreen will make the video and chat cover the entire window.

  • Workmode:

    For when you want to listen to music at work without upsetting the boss man. Workmode will hide the video and chat.

  • Other features:

    We have a few features disabled at the moment because we're waiting for Dubtrack to release their documented API.
